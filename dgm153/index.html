DGM153 Designing the User Experience

Usability testing is a way to see how easy to use something is by testing it with real users. Users are asked to complete tasks, typically while they are being observed by a researcher, to see where they encounter problems and experience confusion.

According to our usability tests, users preferred one of these phone layouts to the others. Click the phones to find out which phone users preferred. Clicking the phone that won our tests will display course information. The others will disappear.

Designing the User Experience introduces students to the foundations of User Experience Design (UX). Students explore the difference between experience design and interface design, create personas, develop scenarios, create paper and electronic prototypes, perform usability tests and create iterative UX designs based on the findings of research with test subjects.

Offered online and blended during Spring semesters.
