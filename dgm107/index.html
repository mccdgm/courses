DGM107 Digital Legalites

Ever wonder how to protect your intellectual property - or for that matter what exactly is intellectual property? If I create something at work is the creation mine or does it belong to my employer? When is it OK to use parts of a published work? What do I do if I find that my work has been used without my permission?

Come join us in Digital Legalities to discover the answers to these and other related topics.

You will have an opportunity to "dream up" your own original creation and advertisement with an eye toward protecting both from the clutches of copyright or patent violators.

Hope to see you in class!

Offered online during Spring and Fall semesters.
